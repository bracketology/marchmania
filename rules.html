March Madness Pool Rules

Entry Rules

  • Entry fee: $10 per bracket
  • Maximum 5 entries per person
  • All brackets must be submitted before the first tournament game begins.
  • The Venmo payment link is located at the bottom of the bracket page.
  • Payment must be completed through Venmo.
  • When sending payment through Venmo, do not mention the pool, gambling, or brackets in the payment note. Keep the caption generic (example: "thanks"). Payments referencing the pool may result in funds being frozen.
  • Entries without payment will be removed.
  • Cancelled entries will be refunded.
  • Please fill out the entry form accurately and use your real name.
  • Entries with fake names or incorrect information will be deleted.
  • Number your entries to help with tracking, as duplicate-named entries will be cancelled. Example: Cooper Flagg #1 or Cooper Flagg 1.
  • If duplicate-named entries are submitted, the soonest-entered bracket will be deleted.

Prize Structure

The site includes a 7.5% rake. Prize percentages below are calculated after rake has been deducted from the total pool.

Points are awarded for each correct pick:

Tiebreaker

Participants must enter a prediction for the total points scored in the championship game. If multiple entries finish with the same score, the bracket closest to the correct total will win the tiebreaker.

General Rules

  • Brackets lock at noon on Thursday.
  • Once submitted, brackets cannot be changed.
  • Points are awarded only for correctly predicted winners.
  • The leaderboard will be updated as games are completed.
  • The commissioner reserves the right to resolve disputes if necessary.
